Basic eligibility checklist

You may qualify if you:

  • Are at least 18 years old.
  • Are a United States resident.
  • Have a verifiable source of income.
  • Have an open checking account that meets the lender’s requirements.

You may not qualify if you:

  • Do not meet the lender’s income requirements.
  • Do not meet checking-account requirements.
  • Are currently in bankruptcy or planning to file.
  • Do not live in an eligible state or cannot complete verification.

What MaxLend says to review

MaxLend’s own materials say that not all applicants will qualify for a loan, and eligibility depends on factors such as age, residency, income, and bank-account status. That means it is important to confirm the latest terms before applying.

Before you apply
