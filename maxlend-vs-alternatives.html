Who MaxLend may fit

Good fit

  • You need emergency cash now.
  • You have bad credit.
  • You want to check eligibility online first.

Probably not a fit

  • You can wait for a lower-cost loan.
  • You have access to cheaper credit.
  • You want the lowest total repayment cost possible.

Decision summary

If speed matters more than cost, MaxLend may deserve a closer look. If your priority is the lowest possible cost, compare every alternative before you apply.
