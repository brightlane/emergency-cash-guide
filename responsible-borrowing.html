Four borrowing rules

1. Borrow less

Only take the amount you actually need, not the maximum available.

2. Compare first

Look at multiple lenders so you understand the total cost and repayment terms.

3. Read everything

Review the agreement carefully before signing or submitting an application.

4. Repay on time

Pay on schedule so you avoid extra fees and protect your borrowing record.

Good signs

  • The lender is transparent about terms and fees.
  • The application process is clear and easy to understand.
  • You have a realistic repayment plan before you borrow.

Red flags

  • Pressure to borrow more than you need.
  • Hidden fees or confusing loan terms.
  • Any claim that approval is guaranteed.
Always verify the latest terms on the lender’s site before applying.
